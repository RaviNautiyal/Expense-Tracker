Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our personal expense tracking application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), and username when you create an account
  • Expense Data: Transaction details including amount, category, date, description, and any notes you add
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the application
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Spendly service
  • Display your expense history and generate summaries
  • Send you service-related communications
  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of passwords using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who assist in operating the application (bound by confidentiality obligations)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and expense history
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your expense data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (may affect service functionality)

7. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses essential cookies to maintain your session and provide core functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request data deletion, your personal information and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com
